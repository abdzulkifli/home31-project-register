PRINCIPLES 03

Enterprise Design Principles

Guardrails to preserve consistency as the platform expands.

One source of truthInitiative records, ownership, decisions and evidence use controlled enterprise data.
Management by exceptionDashboards surface material gaps rather than overwhelming users with raw data.
